User Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

According to Meta platform rules and standard data privacy laws (like GDPR and CCPA), you have the right to request the deletion of your user data. testApp provides two methods for you to manage and delete your information.

Method 1: Remove Access via Meta (Automated)

You can instantly revoke our application's access to your data directly through your Facebook or Instagram account. This is the fastest method to ensure we can no longer access your profile.

  1. Go to your Facebook or Instagram account Settings.
  2. Navigate to the Apps and Websites section.
  3. Locate testApp in the list of active applications.
  4. Click the Remove button next to our app.
  5. Confirm your choice. This will sever the connection and initiate an automated data purge protocol from our active systems.

Method 2: Manual Deletion Request

If you want to ensure that all historical data, logs, and information associated with your account are permanently erased from our databases, you can submit a manual deletion request.

  • Send an email stating your request to delete your data.
  • Include the exact name or email address associated with the account you used to authorize the app so we can locate your records.
  • We will process your request and confirm the deletion within 7 to 14 business days.
